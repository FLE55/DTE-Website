Resilience / Prep

Emergency Grab-Bag Checklist

  • At least 3 liters of water per person
  • Battery-powered radio and extra batteries
  • First aid kit and essential medicines
  • Waterproof torch
  • Sturdy walking shoes
  • 500g of emergency food per person

3 Steps to Secure Your Home

  • Trim high tree branches near your roof.
  • Clear all gutters of leaves and debris.
  • Tape or board up large glass windows.
